## Outlet notes

**JF Insights & Perspectives (JFIP)** — AFA-published sister journal to *Journal of Finance*, submission portal opened January 2026. Slotted in the field tier on current market read: IDEAS / EJMR signal points to "above JFQA, tenure-counts-as-field at most institutions through the late 2020s," with the AFA's "JF-equivalent" framing not yet absorbed by the market. Format constraints: ≤7,000 words main text, ≤5 exhibits (each reduces budget by 200 words), single-insight, in-or-out review (no R&R). Best within-tier match for papers that are tight, single-mechanism, and clean enough to fit. The editor agent should prefer JFIP within field tier when the paper format-fits (see `editor.md` "Journal-fit verdict"). Do not target JFIP for sprawling multi-mechanism papers — JFQA, Review of Finance, and Management Science accommodate longer formats.
