## Outlet notes

**AER Insights** — AEA-published short-format sister journal to *American Economic Review*, launched 2019. Slotted in the field tier on current market read: IDEAS 10-year rank ~21 (sitting with JME and JEEA), WoS JIF ~6.7–8.1, ~4.7% acceptance rate. Tenure treatment is heterogeneous — near-top-5 at the elite institutions whose faculty sit on the editorial board (MIT, Harvard, Stanford, Yale, Princeton), counted-as-field at most other R1s. Format constraints: ≤6,000 words, fast turnaround, "same standards" as AER per AEA. Best within-tier match for papers that are tight, single-mechanism, and clean enough to fit. The editor agent should prefer AER Insights within field tier when the paper format-fits (see `editor.md` "Journal-fit verdict"). Do not target AER Insights for sprawling multi-mechanism papers — JME, AEJ:Macro, and RED accommodate longer formats.
