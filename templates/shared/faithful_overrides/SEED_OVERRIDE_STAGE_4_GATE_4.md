### Faithful-mode override (applies because `faithful: true` in `pipeline_state.json`; supersedes the seeded-mode override)

**Gate 4 REVISE / MAJOR REWORK**: return to Stage 2 with the contract intact. Address only the scorer's **correctness/rigor** comments (math gaps, unclear derivations, missing characterizations). Do NOT adopt scorer suggestions that propose a different framing, mechanism, headline result, or research question — the scorer does not know this is a faithful run, and its publishability-driven recommendations are out of scope by construction. Forward only the correctness-bucket of the scorer's comments to theory-generator; classify the remainder as `[RESPONSE]`-equivalent and log to `process_log/pivot_log.md`.

**Gate 4 ABANDON**: stop and write `output/seed/abandon_report.md` with a post-mortem. Do NOT return to Stage 0 or Stage 1 to pick a different idea.

**Plateau ship rule**: if the score plateaus (no improvement of ≥ 3 across 3 consecutive evaluations) and the contract is intact (no invariant has been violated and the score is being held below threshold by aspects that are *outside the developer's control under the contract* — e.g., novelty when the seed's contribution is already KNOWN, surprise when the seed's stated result is OBVIOUS, scope when the seed intentionally constrains scope), proceed to Stage 5 and document the score ceiling in `output/seed/limitations.md`. The deepening playbook is allowed only insofar as it produces *deeper analysis of the contract's mechanism*; introducing model-structure changes (continuous time, heterogeneity, learning, alternative preferences) as the *baseline* model that the contract does not name is a rescope and is forbidden. **A robustness appendix that uses a model-structure variation to verify the contract's main result is allowed** if it supplements (does not replace) the baseline model — e.g., if the contract names a representative-agent baseline, an appendix showing the result survives in a heterogeneous-agent extension is an addition, not a rescope. The line is whether the contract's named baseline remains the paper's primary object; structural variations as robustness checks are additions on top.

**Escalation rules**: the "5 theories → Stage 0" and "delta < 3 → escalate one level" rules do NOT apply in faithful mode in a way that swaps the idea or rescopes the mechanism. Branch-manager RESTART/REGENERATE recommendations are classified `[RESPONSE]` and logged.
